MENTORSHIP

TESTIMONIALS   MENTORSHIP APPLICATION  FAQ 

The mentorship program at Yoga Vida provides 200 hour graduates with a platform to continue their studies. It strives to create a bridge between foundational training and teaching by gaining experience conducting classes, refining teaching skills and studying directly with one of our senior instructors. This program is designed to make participants strong, confident teachers who can lead a room and create an environment conducive to learning. It is open to anyone who holds a 200 hour Teacher Training certificate.

    HISTORY & PHILOSOPHY CURRICULUM:     
    What is the connection between postural yoga as it is commonly practiced in contemporary New York with pre-modern yoga traditions of South Asia?  Is modern posture focused yoga practice a unique tradition?  

    In the philosophy component of the mentorship program, we will explore these questions in an attempt to more fully understand the history of transnational modern yoga practice.  We will pay particular attention to the tradition of Krishnamacharya in recognition of the enormous impact made by his students, specifically B.K.S. Iyengar and K. Pattabhi Jois, on the asana focused yoga practice so popular today.
